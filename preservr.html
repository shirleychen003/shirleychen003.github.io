OVERVIEW

While past generations may have used physical photo albums and mediums to leave their legacies, our digital-native generation uses platforms such as Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook to illustrate and tell their personal stories.

Preservr offers an integrated tool suite that empowers individuals to safeguard their digital footprints by addressing organization, accessibility, sustainability, and cultural heritage, enabling people to curate and preserve meaningful content for future generations.

INITIAL IDEATION
PERSONA STATEMENT

Understanding the Problem

Users face the risk of losing their digital identities and narratives due to:

platform obsolescence
data and privacy
risks
evolving digital landscapes
TURNING INSIGHTS INTO ACTION

So how did we address these issues? After conducting thorough research about the problem space, we followed a 3-step approach to develop and implement our solution. This allowed the team to manage complexity and ensure a cohesive integration of research, technical development, and user experience.

Archival Documentation

This process consisted of creating workflow documents and flowcharts for social media platforms to simplify the data download process and developing a glossary to help users understand and navigate their data packages.

Narrowing the scope to solely Instagram, any documents were then integrated into the project website as searchable PDFs, serving as a guide for users to obtain their Instagram data.

Instagram Data Archival Analysis

Using Python and exported JSON files of Instagram data, scripts and a user-friendly GUI in Tkinter were developed for local-use. This way, users were able to take uninterpretable data and convert it to a comprehensible form.

Website Building

To properly display all our work, our group built a website using Wordpress to act as a central hub for all research documents and created tools from the project. Each tool is showcased on each webpage and include the aforementioned instruction documents for support.

TAKEAWAYS
