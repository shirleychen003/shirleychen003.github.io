1. Background

For my INF352: Information Design Studio II: How to Design class, one of the final assignments was to submit a website portfolio imitating what I would potentially submit if I were applying to UX designer roles. I had little experience in development, so I quickly turned to using the website builder SquareSpace. While the application was intuitive and easy to use, I felt that it offered limited flexibility in terms of customization beyond the provided templates. With encouragement from my peers, I set the objective to design and develop my own website.
